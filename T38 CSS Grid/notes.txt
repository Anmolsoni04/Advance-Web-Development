Grid is used to make the different and stylish layouts!

gird-template-columns: xyz px; (This property sorts the content columnwise)

grid line and grid cell

horizontal and vertical lines un a grid is known as grid lines

small boxes between 2 lines is known as grid cell

grid track and grid area

space between 2 grid lines either horizontal OR Vertical is known as grid track

area between 2 lines is known as grid area

Grid templates row and columns

 grid-row: 1/2; OR we can also write this as:
 grid-row-start: 1
 grid-row-end: 2
